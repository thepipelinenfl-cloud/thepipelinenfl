# Privacy Policy

## The Pipeline Edge

**Last Updated: April 2026**

---

## Overview

The Pipeline Edge ("App") is an informational tool for confidence pool and pickem players. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

---

## Data We Collect

**Data You Provide (Not Personal)**
- Your weekly picks (stored on your device)
- Your pool results (wins/losses each week)
- Your season performance history
- Game selections and confidence rankings

**Automatically Collected**
- App crash reports
- Feature usage (which tabs you use)
- Basic analytics (no personal identifiers)

**Optional Data (Account Only)**
- Email address (only if you create an account)
- Cloud backup of your picks (if enabled)

---

## Data Storage & Security

- All pick data stored locally on YOUR device
- We do not store your personal information on our servers
- Optional account data is encrypted
- We do not sell or share your data

---

## Third-Party Services

- Google Analytics (usage only, no personal data)
- Apple/Google crash reporting

## ⚠️ Important: NOT a Betting App

This app does NOT:
- Facilitate gambling or wagering
- Connect to sportsbooks
- Provide odds or betting advice
- Promote real-money betting

*The app is for informational and entertainment purposes only.*
